What do we do?
We aim to help you find the best health insurance with the most value.
How do we do it?
By utilizing health insurance data and your basic information anonymously, we use advanced algorithms and data analytics to calculate your yearly healthcare expenses and recommend the best plan for you based on the lowest expected total annual cost.
How are we different?
Our solution helps you make the best decision by providing transparency regarding health insurance. We are highly focused on user experience, both on mobile tablets and on desktop. We have talented engineers and designers with experience working at Google, LinkedIn, and Cisco. We also have experts in the field as our mentors, and we're looking to solve our own problem to empower consumers to find the best plan with the greatest value.
